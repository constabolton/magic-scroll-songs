Stay All Night (Stay A Little Longer) 
~ Bob Wills & His Texas Playboy

The lyrics and verse order were taken from the movie clip from Lawless Empire (a 1945 Durango Kid movie).  In 
the clip the song is titled "Stay A Little Longer" although credits show the song title as "Stay All Night, 
Stay a Little Longer."  The recording from "The Tiffany Transcriptions Vol. 5" swap the second and third 
verse below.
