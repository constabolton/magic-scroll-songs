**Hello and welcome to Magic Scroll!**

I'm Spencer, I'm the developer of this app (Friends call me Sunny! Either way I'm still the developer of the app). Thank you for giving it a try!

Magic Scroll is a platform for anyone interested in making music, *from hobbyists to songwriters,* to format and share the songs they want to play, however they want to play them. It's totally _ad-free,_ so _no subscriptions,_ _pop-ups,_ or _premium features_ can bother you when you just want to share some tunes with your band or strum a little bit with your ukulele group.

If you want to get started, you can access the "Library" sidebar to add your own music, search for new ones, or organise the music you already have! If you're at a loss for what to try, check out our pre-made collections full of traditional tunes and folk bangers!

What you're looking at right now is technically a song/chord sheet. These are usually formatted by tagging each chord to the exact word or syllable it belongs to — click **Add Chords** above the editor, then click a word to drop a chord right on it:

[C]Here are [F]some examples of [G]lyrics I could [Am]type
[Am9]Here are [Dm11sus4]some needlessly overwrought [Gmaj7add9]chords

(Classic two-line chord sheets, with the chords on their own line above the lyrics, still work too. The "Add Chords" button will even convert one to the tagged format for you automatically.)

You can transpose these chords with the buttons available in the top bar, as well as simplify more difficult chords depending on your skill level. These options work across all types of music, so your sheet music and lead sheets can be transposed and simplified as well. If you're not interested in playing guitar, you can select chord diagrams for plenty of other instruments and tuning systems!

Finally, if you have any questions, comments, concerns, or requests, you can always just ask me! My contact detail is available in the tab on the bottom left corner, as well as the FAQ and credits.

Have fun!
