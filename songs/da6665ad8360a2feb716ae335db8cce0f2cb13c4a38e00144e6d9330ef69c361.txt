Song name: Goodbye  by  Mary Hopkin
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This is Mary Hopkin’s Goodbye. When you play, you want to be sure to have a
capo on the 4th or 5th fret. They both sound good, though. If you want to do it
like Paul McCartney did for the acetate demo, do not use a capo. It is also
easier to sing it with no capo. If you feel that there are any corrections, feel
free to correct. Enjoy!
