PLEASE NOTE: 
Although every other source has the 2nd verse ending
'in bloom', I believe it is actually 'in blue',
rhyming with 'you'. This makes much more sense in the 
context of the whole song being about his loved one
having to leave for a while. Even in live videos it
isn't distinct enough, so please make your own choice

For the G to C/G changes, play the G chord using the
middle, ring and little fingers, then the C/G by
moving the middle finger across to the 4th string
and adding the other note on the 2nd string with your
index finger. Elsewhere the G chord can be played as
usual with the index, middle and ring fingers.
Emphasise the bass note of each chord.

I would recommend using the voicings in the text
column numbers below.
Regarding the CHORD BOX DIAGRAMS please follow the
information at the bottom of the tab
