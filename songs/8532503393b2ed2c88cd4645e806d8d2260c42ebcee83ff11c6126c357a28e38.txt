During the intro, the chords surrounded by *'s are an optional way to simulate the piano part in the song. 
The main chord is C#m and the other two are played only once in fast succession. the strum pattern is
down, down up down up down, down, down with the last two down strums being the second two chords.

If you listen for the piano part in the song it's clear. When I play this song I replace all of the 
fills with this lick, because frankly, I think it sounds AMAZING.
