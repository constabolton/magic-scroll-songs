This song seems to exist with two names - Be Kind To Your Parents & the similar Be Kind To Your Parent.

Parent song from "Young Vs Old" album - https://youtu.be/DPz9TpTG6HY

Parents song from "For Kids And Just Plain Folks" album - https://youtu.be/gGO1JdLMLx8

They are the exact same song - the "Parents" version was released in 1967 and without the plural in 1969.

It would seem that when it was re-issued someone at the record label erroneously dropped the plural
