----------------------------------------------------------------------
Song name: Dream A Little Dream Of Me  by  Cass Elliot
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Dream a Little Dream of Me. It was first recorded in February 1931 by Ozzie Nelson and 
also by Wayne King. The music was composed by Fabian Andre and Wilbur Schwandt and the Lyric
by Gus Kahn in 1931. However, the highest chart ratings were in 1968 when  it was performed by 
Mama Cass Elliot with The Mamas & the Papas. This chords version is from the recorded version
of Cass Elliot.
